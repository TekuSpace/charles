About Me

Hello! I am Charles Isaya, a dedicated Mechanical Engineer at Mbeya University of Science and Technology (MUST). With over a decade of experience in the field, I have developed expertise automotive car mechanics, modern cars.

I hold a Ordinary Diploma in Mechanical Engineering from Mbeya University of Science and Technology. My research focuses on Car energy systems, advanced car materials, etc. and I am passionate about applying engineering principles to solve real-world problems.

In addition to my academic work, I have collaborated on numerous projects with industry partners, contributing to innovations in automotive. I am also committed to mentoring students and fostering a culture of excellence in engineering education.
